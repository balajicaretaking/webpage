About Balaji Caretaking

Your trusted partner for dedicated and skilled caretaking & ward-maid assistance.

Welcome to Balaji Caretaking, where we deliver exceptional and compassionate ward-maid and caretaking services directly to your home. Our proprietor, Ms. Dhanalaxmi Ramu Chaudhary, brings years of hands-on experience and heartfelt dedication to patient well-being.

We understand the importance of trust and reliability. Our mission is to provide personalised assistance that meets each client’s unique needs, fostering a supportive and healing environment. We proudly serve Kamala Nehru Nagar, Sion, Dharavi, and neighbouring areas of Mumbai.

Whether you need post-operative support, elder companionship, or day-to-day ward-maid help, Balaji Caretaking is here with professionalism and empathy.

Our Comprehensive Services

Tailored caretaking solutions to support your health and recovery.

Attendant / Ward-Maid Services

Our trained ward maids take care of daily chores, hygiene and patient comfort, following strict COVID-19 precautions.

  • Oral feeding
  • Timely medicines
  • Back care
  • Basic exercises
  • Nebulisation
  • Laundry assistance

Elder Care & Companionship

Specially trained caretakers provide companionship, mobility support, and everyday assistance to seniors.

  • Friendly companionship
  • Help with bathing & hygiene
  • Counselling & conversation
  • Accompanied walks

Caretaking – ICU & Non-ICU

Skilled ward maids work alongside medical staff, ensuring constant attention and comfort for critical or recovering patients.

  • Vitals monitoring & documentation
  • Catheter & tubing assistance
  • Sponging & diaper change
  • Medication reminders
  • Ryles-tube feeding
  • Injection preparation support
  • Dressing change assistance
  • Mobility & basic physiotherapy

Critical Care Assistance
